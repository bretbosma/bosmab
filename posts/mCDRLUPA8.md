**mCDRLUPA8** Testing of LUPA in the large wave flume (LWF) with electrolyzer.  LUPA charges batteries that suupply power to the electrolyzer.

Duration: Spring 2026

Facility: LWF

Conditions tested: Regular and Random waves just charging capacitors and charging batteries

Goals:

* Demonstrate LUPA powering mCDR electrolyzer
    + Characterize and find max electrical power conditions for LUPA in the LWF
    + Damping sweep finding otpimal damping for electrical power charging capacitors
    + Testing of random wave electrical power generation for two Tp values finding max Hs condition
    + Test charging of the batteries with charge controller
